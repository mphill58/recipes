Ingredients:
1. Veggies 
	a. snap peas
	b. carrots
	c. broccoli
	d. peppers (I prefer hot)
	e. any others to your fancy
2. chicken (or beef or tofu)
3. soy sauce or similar 
4. butter (something to grease the pan, veggie oil)

Directions:
1. heat the pan to medium heat (~5 if your dial goes to 10)
2. add the butter/oil
3. add veggies, cut to your liking (I like mine diced small)
	a. cook for 10 minutes
4. Add meat (seasoned and diced into bite-sized cubes)
	a. cook till done 
5. Add soy sauce
6. Enjoy!
